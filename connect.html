Meet some of our fine staff!

Robbie Myers is a film student at Western Kentucky University. His hometown is Louisville, although he lived in a small-town in California until he was 14 years old. His passions include film, coffee, and music. His favorite film is Star Wars, which he insists can be made relevant to all topics of conversation. Robbie is also very interested in China and the relationship it has with the United States. Through his involvement at Project Pengyou, he hopes to nurture his skills in filmmaking by using film as a means to facilitate discussion that leads to better US-China relations.
